Useful fields:

- **Primary audience's prior beliefs about the data**
- **What they want the analysis to surface** (sometimes vs. what the data actually supports)
- **Sensitivities** — topics to handle carefully (e.g., personnel-level findings, comparison to other programs)
- **Preferred framing** — language that resonates, language that lands wrong
- **Decisions they're trying to make** — what the analysis must answer, vs. what's interesting but not actionable
